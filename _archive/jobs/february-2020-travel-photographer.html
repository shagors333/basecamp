The world's best job?

We think so.

Here at Trek View we use 360-degree imagery to help protect people and the planet. Think Google Street View for the natural world.

We're currently looking for a travel photographer on an initial 6 month contract to help us capture 360-degree tours for commercial clients around the world.

Rainforests, mountaintops and beaches await.

You provide the passport and energy. We'll provide the equipment, accommodation, and all travel expenses, on top of a salary of $350 USD per week.

No previous photography experience is required. Budding smartphone photographers are welcome to apply.

Though this job is not for everyone.

We need someone who is:

  • Prepared to be on the road (and in the air) for the 6 month period, starting in May/June 2020.
  • Relatively fit. You will be covering up to 15 kilometres of uneven terrain on day by foot.
  • In possession of a valid passport and is able to travel to Africa, Europe, and North and South America (we will fund required visa applications).
  • Fluent in English. Ability to speak Spanish is a plus.

If you're planning a gap year and have a sense of adventure, this job is perfect.

To apply, send us an email telling us why you're the adventurer we're looking for.
