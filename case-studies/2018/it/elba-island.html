Elba Smart Exploring was a project commissioned by Visit Elba to enable anyone with a smartphone to explore the island's outdoor trails.

The island, housing over 200 beaches and 350 km of trails, wanted to focus it's marketing towards outdoor adventure based holidaymakers.

By mixing Street View 360 imagery with dedicated online websites and content, the aim was to give tourists planning their visits the tools to plan their excursions by choosing and building personalised itineraries, from the comfort of their sofa.

The Elba Smart Exploring website allows you to choose your hike and start your virtual experition.

The project recieved much press attention, consequently helping to drive a 10% increase in tourism during the second half of 2018, measured by ferry tickets to the island (the only way on and off).

The project was presented by Federico Debetto, Head of Photography, at the 2018 Google Street View in Mountain View.

About Elba Smart Exploring

Elba Exploring Smart was the first systematic mapping of the island, commissioned by Visit Elba - Associated Management for Elba Island tourism.

The project is in line with a "strategy", that of Elba Active launched a few years ago, which sees the island increasingly projected towards an integrated idea of ​​outdoor activities, healthy food and uncontaminated nature to find its own well-being psychophysical. In the small, large island everyone can in fact create their own "style" of vacation, discover the right size of their active personal well-being . For the season, the Island of Elba will increasingly be the open-air gym dedicated to those who are looking for a place to get fit and get closer to physical activity, in short, find the balance between body and mind.
